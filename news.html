Mode of Order:

1 Upon ordering, the buyer provides full name, address and phone#.

2 With these data JungleJewels applies for DENR Local Transport Permit.

3 Once DENR issues the LTP, a soft copy will be sent to the buyer as proof of legitimacy.

4 At that point the buyer pays 10% downpayment.

5 JungleJewels applies for Veterinary health certificate, shipping permit with the Bureau of Quarantine and the Deed of sale.

6 The remaining balance of 90% must be paid upon personal pickup or before the animals are being shipped.

7 Despite using the best possible packaging and fast shipping, JungleJewels is not responsible for damage or loss during shipping. Personal pickup is highly recommended.
