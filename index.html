Welcome to

Jungle Jewels

Wildlife Farm

For more than 30 years keeping and breeding of exotic animals has been our passion. Located in Leyte, Philippines, we take full advantage of the local climate and keep our reptiles and birds outdoor all year round, where they enjoy natural sun light and seasonal changes of temperature and rainfall. In compliance with local laws, JungleJewels is a registered Wildlife Farm under DENR. We hope you enjoy our website and get interested in this wonderful hobby.
